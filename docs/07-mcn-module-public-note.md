# MCN Module Public Note

The first public industry direction for Vesper is MCN and cross-border e-commerce operations.

This note explains the public, non-sensitive direction of the module.

It does not include customer data, raw interviews, business plans, private workflows, or production implementation details.

## Why MCN Operations?

MCN and cross-border e-commerce operations contain many structured business entities.

Examples include:

- Creators
- Products
- Campaigns
- Videos
- Orders
- Reports
- Tasks

These entities are useful for testing whether an AI Agent Operating System can support real operational workflows.

## Operational Complexity

MCN operations are not only about generating content.

They often involve:

- Matching creators with products
- Planning campaigns
- Tracking video production
- Managing product information
- Following order and sales signals
- Creating operational reports
- Assigning and tracking tasks
- Coordinating between people and business systems

This makes MCN operations a useful first scenario for testing object-based workflows.
